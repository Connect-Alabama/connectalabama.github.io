About Us

Our Mission Statement

Connect Alabama is organized and operated exclusively for charitable purposes in accordance with Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. More specifically, Connect Alabama is dedicated to reducing inequality in computer literacy, technology access, and/or education among youth in Alabama, including, but not limited to, youth in rural Alabama.

What We Do

As part of serving state residents, Connect Alabama collects and refurbishes obsolete technology donated to the organization to provide to other charitable organizations, along with providing information to assist those who need help in adapting to the emerging digital world.

Our Skillset

Our team is CompTIA Security+ certified, and is able to ensure that data on donated machines is irretrievable - our team can also provide a free hardware backup of data, if there are machines you would like to donate that still contain important files. We are based in the Huntsville area, and can provide in-person pickups and dropoffs.
